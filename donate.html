How you can take action:

Short-term solutions: building wells and spreading awareness about why having access to clean water is so important.

Long-term solutions: investing in sustainable infrastructure and advocating for your community to prioritize making clean water available for everyone.

Here are two organizations that you can donate to:

Charity: Water is a non-profit organization whose mission is to provide all people with access to clean water, hygeine, and improved sanitation. Up to date, they have funded 189,000 projects and done work in 29 countries. They work with local partnerships and have a space on their website to make donations.

Water for Life charity is a non-profit organization whose mission is to improve health, educuation, and economic prosperity of families by providing solutions to access clean water. On their website you can sponser projects and donate.
